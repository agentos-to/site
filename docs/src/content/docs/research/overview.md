AgentOS is built on a long chain of prior thinking about knowledge
representation, personal information management, and human-computer
collaboration. This section collects the research we consulted or
produced while shaping the ontology, identity model, and integration
patterns.

Unlike the guides, these pages are exploratory — some reach firm
conclusions, some map the terrain, some are open questions. They are
published so the reasoning is public, not hidden in a private roadmap.

The subsections group roughly by concern:

- **Ontology** — relationship modeling, entity primitives, schema
  vocabularies. Start with [relationship modeling](/docs/research/ontology/relationship-modeling/),
  then browse [Schema.org](/docs/research/ontology/schema-entities/),
  [OGP](/docs/research/ontology/open-graph-protocol/), and
  [genealogy](/docs/research/ontology/genealogical-relationships/).
- **Platforms** — how specific products structure their own entity
  graphs: [Google Takeout](/docs/research/platforms/google-takeout/),
  [Facebook Graph](/docs/research/platforms/facebook-graph/),
  [FamilySearch](/docs/research/platforms/familysearch/).
- **Identity & spaces** — how accounts map to people
  ([account ↔ platform ontology](/docs/research/identity-and-spaces/account-platform-ontology/)),
  how [digital spaces](/docs/research/identity-and-spaces/digital-spaces-ontology/) are
  modeled across platforms, and the [identity standards](/docs/research/identity-and-spaces/platform-identity-standards/)
  landscape.
- **Relationships & events** — [claims](/docs/research/relationships-and-events/claims-relationship-model/),
  [causal chains](/docs/research/relationships-and-events/causal-chains-entity-graphs/),
  [events vs. work](/docs/research/relationships-and-events/event-vs-work-ontology/),
  [speech acts as primitives](/docs/research/relationships-and-events/speech-acts-entity-primitives/).
- **Context & ecosystem** — the surrounding technical and cultural terrain:
  [PKM community](/docs/research/context/pkm-community/),
  [semantic filesystems](/docs/research/context/semantic-file-systems/),
  [AI text editing](/docs/research/context/ai-text-editing/),
  the [Markdown ecosystem](/docs/research/context/markdown-ecosystem/).
- **DX patterns** — [SDK and plugin ergonomics](/docs/research/dx-patterns/sdk-dx-patterns/),
  [simplification strategy](/docs/research/dx-patterns/simplification-passes-accreted-cruft/).
